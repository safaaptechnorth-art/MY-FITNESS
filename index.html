About Us

Prime Fitness is a fitness club established in 1982. The club is equipped with all basic fitness equipment and also includes newly introduced machines. The Prime Fitness Club is the flagship brand for Prime Fitness Club Ltd, a UN-based health and fitness services provider and a pioneer in the “Integrated Health Club Management Solutions” business. Prime Fitness Club Ltd focuses on staying fit as a key to meeting life’s challenges.
